## About Me

I am a master's student in AI and computer vision, with research interests in biometric recognition, image restoration, and deployment-oriented deep learning systems.

My work focuses on:
- Finger-vein recognition and image outpainting
- Knowledge distillation for lightweight vision models
- Deepfake detection and robust visual classification
- Semantic segmentation for autonomous driving
- Edge AI deployment with embedded platforms and inference optimization

I am particularly interested in building vision models that are not only accurate, but also practical for real-world environments such as embedded systems and resource-constrained deployment scenarios.

## Featured Projects

### [KD-AFA-Net](https://github.com/junseo0421/KD-AFA-Net)
Deep learning-based finger-vein image outpainting project implemented in PyTorch, based on knowledge distillation and adaptive feature attention for biometric image restoration.

### [diffusion-fingervein-spoof-detection](https://github.com/junseo0421/diffusion-fingervein-spoof-detection)
A mini-project on synthetic finger-vein image generation using diffusion models, where DDPM-generated spoof samples are used for real/fake anti-spoofing classification.

### [deepfake-detection-swinv2-effort](https://github.com/junseo0421/deepfake-detection-swinv2-effort)
A deepfake detection pipeline based on Swin Transformer V2 with EFFORT-style SVD-residual fine-tuning, developed for the 2025 Hecto AI Challenge.  
**Leaderboard:** Top 6.7% (**31 / 458**)

### [2025-autonomous-driving-ai-challenge-semseg](https://github.com/junseo0421/2025-autonomous-driving-ai-challenge-semseg)
Semantic segmentation project for autonomous driving, combining DDRNet-based training, teacher-student knowledge distillation, degradation-based augmentation, and rare-class-aware oversampling.

### [short_kinds](https://github.com/short-kinds)
A news big data hackathon project developed in an organization repository, selected as a finalist-level team entry in the 2025 News Big Data Hackathon.
